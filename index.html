About us

Enhancing Child Safety at Daycares

At SafeDrop Solution, we are dedicated to providing an innovative software platform that ensures the safety and security of children during daycare drop-off times. Our system is designed with one goal in mind: to prevent tragic incidents involving children left in vehicles. SafeDrop Solution offers an intuitive interface that streamlines the drop-off process, giving parents and daycare staff peace of mind. Our service includes a comprehensive setup by our expert team, tailored to fit the unique needs of your daycare center. Key features of SafeDrop Solution include:

•Customizable Drop-Off Tracking: Monitor and manage drop-offs with ease, ensuring every child is accounted for at the start of the day.
•Affordable Pricing: With a monthly subscription starting at $80, SafeDrop Solution is an accessible and valuable investment in child safety.
•Scalability: Our pricing model is designed to grow with your daycare, with customized options available for centers with more than 100 children.


SafeDrop Solution is more than just software—it’s a commitment to safety and a tool that helps prevent child deaths in hot vehicles. Join us in creating safer daycare environments today.
